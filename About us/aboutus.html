About Us

What We Do:


Welcome to Thrift Fashion Store , your number one source for all things product, ie: shoes, bags, cloths etc. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of product, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2020 by Alex Jennifer, Thrift Fashion Store has come a long way from its beginnings in a starting location, ie: online platform for the time of Covid .

When Alex Jennifer first started out, his passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly, providing the best quality product and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store.

We now serve customers all over place The world and are thrilled to be a part of the adjective, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How we were established:


We were established when we realized that it was necessary to fix our traffic system and start awareness for traffic laws. As we are all aware our system isn't efficient. We adviced a plan to fix it and make this project.
